Community Monetary Intermediation

ISIC 6419 · maturity implemented

Problem: Banking operators can't deploy their own settlement and interbank-message workflows without surrendering control to closed SaaS or building compliance scaffolding from scratch.

Solution: Governed open-source blueprint for community monetary intermediation — account intake, AML/KYC compliance, sanctions screening, settlement posting, and interbank-message dispatch — with an independent Governor that holds unsafe commits and routes high-stakes decisions to human approval.

What it is not: This actor does not claim to hold banking licenses or directly connect to SWIFT/ISO 20022 clearing networks; you supply jurisdiction-specific licensing and core-banking integrations.
